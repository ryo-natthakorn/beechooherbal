## 2. Phase 4 anomaly log (redirect / SEO landmines)

Carry these into the redirect map + hreflang build. Sources: `inventory/parity-report.md`,
`inventory/orphan-reconciliation.md`, `inventory/events-dupe-check.md`.

- **Thai home:** `/th/` **301 → `/th/home/`**. Thai home is `/th/home/`, not `/th/`.
- **Broken URLs — fix, do NOT faithfully copy:**
  - `/5-causes-of-hair-loss-…/` 301-loops into a doubled path that ends at a **`.jpg`**. Recover the
    post body separately; don't migrate the redirect.
  - `/suffering-from-alopecia-…/` returns **500**.
- **WP REST `posts` endpoint 500s for every variant** → blog/event **post** bodies must be **HTML-scraped**
  in Phase 4 (REST **pages** works: 18 EN / 17 TH).
- **Events archive vs page (decided):** `301 /category/events-news/ → /events-news-release/` as a
  **one-off** (both are in the sitemap, self-canonical, near-duplicate). **Keep `/category/blog/`** (it's
  the blog index). Do **NOT** use a blanket `/category/*` wildcard — it would wrongly hit the blog index.
- **Dead hreflang targets — do NOT reproduce:** `/th/author/admin/` and `/th/category/blog-th/` are
  referenced by live hreflang but are not real crawlable pages. (No `/th/category/...` exists in the sitemap.)
- **Near-duplicate / mislabeled slug:** `/grand-opening-…ประเวศ…-2567-2/` has a Prawet slug but its
  **title is actually Chatuchak** (reused slug). Pair event posts by **title**, not slug, in Phase 4.
- **Canonical drift:** several root Thai posts self-report `canonical` at a non-existent `/th/<slug>/`.
- **Orphans are a Crispin decision, not auto-fix:** 30 EN-only (incl. Privacy Policy, the
  `/category/events-news/` archive, a Male-Pattern-Baldness post that is genuinely Thai-less) and 26
  Thai-only root posts. Never machine-translate a missing page. See `inventory/orphans-for-crispin.md`.
